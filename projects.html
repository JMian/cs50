1. A mini-game I made on Scratch:

Little Penguin Red Diamond

Instruction

Click the green flag to start/restart to game.
Your ultimate goal is to get the red diamond.
You need three keys to summon it.
Use the arrow keys to move your character during the game.
Further instructions and information are provided throughout the game.

Notes

The original project can be found at https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/314889807/. You are of course welcome to leave any comments you might have!
